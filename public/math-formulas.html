Mathematics Formulas

A curated collection of essential formulas from various branches of mathematics.

Algebra

Quadratic Formula

x = [-b ± √(b²-4ac)] / 2a

Solves any quadratic equation of the form ax² + bx + c = 0.

Binomial Theorem

(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

Expands the square of a sum of two terms.

Geometry

Pythagorean Theorem

a² + b² = c²

Relates the sides of a right-angled triangle, where c is the hypotenuse.

Area of a Circle

A = πr²

Calculates the area (A) of a circle with radius (r).

Circumference of a Circle

C = 2πr

Calculates the distance around a circle with radius (r).

Calculus

Power Rule for Derivatives

d/dx (xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹

A fundamental rule for finding the derivative of a variable raised to a power.

Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

∫ₐᵇ f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)

Connects the concepts of differentiating a function with integrating a function.
