Physics Formulas

A collection of fundamental formulas that govern the laws of the physical world.

Mechanics

Newton's Second Law

F = ma

The force (F) on an object is equal to its mass (m) multiplied by its acceleration (a).

Kinetic Energy

KE = ½mv²

Calculates the kinetic energy of an object with mass (m) and velocity (v).

Electromagnetism

Ohm's Law

V = IR

Relates voltage (V), current (I), and resistance (R) in an electrical circuit.

Coulomb's Law

F = k * |q₁q₂| / r²

Calculates the electrostatic force between two charged particles.

Thermodynamics

Ideal Gas Law

PV = nRT

Describes the state of an amount of gas under given conditions of pressure (P), volume (V), and temperature (T).
